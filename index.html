Roth Conversion Readiness Quiz

1. Do you expect your tax rate to be higher in retirement than it is today?

2. How will you pay the taxes on a Roth conversion?

3. When do you plan to start withdrawing from retirement accounts?

4. How important is it for you to reduce Required Minimum Distributions (RMDs) later in life?

5. How do you expect your investments to perform in the coming years?

6. What percentage of your retirement savings is in pre-tax accounts (401k, Traditional IRA)?

7. How important is leaving tax-free assets to heirs?

8. Do you anticipate any unusually high-income years soon?

9. How comfortable are you with paying taxes now to save more later?

10. Have you explored Roth conversions with a financial professional before?
